Our Story

The year was 1982. Bruce Wayne and Alfred Pennyworth had recently moved to Toronto from Vancouver, BC. All was fine until one day when the two were craving wings. Not just ordinary wings, but authentic Buffalo, New York-style chicken wings. With none to be found nearby, Bruce and Alfred had two choices: road trip to Vancouver, or open a wing joint close to home. Lucky for us, they chose the latter. Hence, the beginning of Wings Dings, the welcoming neighborhood atmosphere with a front-row seat for every sports fan that offers 21 mouth-watering signature sauces and seasonings.
